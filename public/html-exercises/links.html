Top 3 movie picks

Predestinationfalls under the third spot of my top 3 movies. It is just amazing how the story of 3 different characters were stitched to one, cohesively. Also, I can say that the story and the plot of the movie was very much taken thought of. To be able to produce such bizzare story through a film is just outstanding.

Second, is the animated movie Meet the Robinsons. The first time I have seen it in Disney channel when I was a kid, I was just instantly hooked up to it. I love how it focused on family, and especially on hoping for a brighter tomorrow and just "keep moving forward."

And the Harry Potter series falls on the top spot! I just love the idea of being a wizard, going to Hogwarts, embarking on an adventure with friends, and battling against a multitude of villains overnight, risking your life.

Top 3 songs

Third on the list is, "Feeling Good" by Nina Simone. I like this because it sounds really old, well it's a really old song. It just have that hope in it, like there's a new chance to make everything right or better.

On the second spot, "Parting Glass" specifically the cover of Ed Sheeran. I just love how haunting it sounds, and how great it is to be an ending song for a gig.

"Lego House" tops the list! It is because it's the first song by Ed Sheeran that I have fell in love with. Basically, being an avid Harry Potter fan, back in 2011, I accidentally was hooked to it because of Rupert Grint, assuming that he has changed his name and started a singing career. I then came to know the truth and since then I have been a fan of Ed Sheeran.
